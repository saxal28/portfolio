Why Web Development?

One morning, as I was getting dressed for a job that I hated, I had a sudden burst of clarity. "What am I doing? Why am I working a job that I have no passion for?"

I've always been curious about programming/web development and about computers/technology in general; however, until my epiphany, I had never actually chased my passions. From the moment that I started learning syntax, I have been glued to my computer screen. I love how I can create something totally unique and full of purpose out of nothing. It's the ultimate expression of creative freedom. I enjoy working with code and I always strive to create new applications and improve my skills.
